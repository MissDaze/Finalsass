Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sassy Vintage Tees (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses and protects your personal information when you visit our website and purchase our products. We are based in Australia and aim to handle your information in line with the Australian Privacy Principles. [web:15][web:53]

1. Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our site:

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, shipping address and phone number when you place an order or join our email list.
  • Order information such as items purchased, payment method (via our payment provider), order value and transaction date.
  • Technical data such as your IP address, browser type, device information and pages you visit, collected through cookies or similar technologies.

2. How we use your information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders, including sending order confirmations and shipping updates.
  • Respond to your questions, requests and customer service messages.
  • Send marketing emails if you have opted in (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • Operate, maintain and improve our website and product offering.
  • Comply with our legal and tax obligations.

3. Payments and third‑party services

We use Stripe to process payments. Your card details are handled directly by Stripe and are not stored on our servers. Stripe may process your data in other countries; you can read their privacy practices on the Stripe website. [web:10][web:52]

We may also use email marketing or analytics services that process limited personal data in order to send newsletters or understand how people use our site.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and understand how you use the site. You can change your browser settings to refuse cookies, but some parts of the site may not function properly if you do.

5. Disclosure of your information

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers such as payment processors, email platforms, hosting providers and shipping/courier companies, but only to the extent needed to perform their services.
  • Professional advisers (for example, accountants or legal advisers) where reasonably necessary.
  • Authorities or regulators where we are legally required to do so.

We do not sell your personal information.

6. International transfers

Some of our service providers may store or process data outside Australia. Where possible, we choose reputable providers that use appropriate safeguards to protect your information.

7. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss and unauthorised access, modification or disclosure, including by using secure connections and limiting access to authorised personnel only. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

8. How long we keep your information

We keep your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes set out in this policy, to comply with legal and tax requirements or to resolve disputes.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link in our emails.

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their policies.
