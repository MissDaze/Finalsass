Pre‑order Information

Last updated: February 2026

1. What is a pre‑order?

A pre‑order means you are purchasing a product that hasn’t been printed yet. Once the pre‑order window closes, we send the total quantities and sizes to our local printer and produce the tees in a single batch. This helps us reduce waste and keep the drop limited. [web:16]

2. Current drop timeline

  • Pre‑order opens: February 2026
  • Pre‑order closes: End of February 2026
  • Printing scheduled: Immediately after pre‑orders close
  • Expected dispatch: Second week of March 2026

These dates are our best estimates and may change due to printing or courier delays. If there is a significant delay, we will email you with an updated estimate and options (including a refund if the new timeframe doesn’t work for you).

3. Payment

Payment for pre‑orders is taken at the time you place your order via Stripe. This secures your item in the print run. [web:10][web:12]

4. Changes and cancellations

If you need to update your shipping address or contact details, please email us as soon as possible. Once your order has gone to print, we may not be able to change the size or colour.

If there is a substantial delay beyond the expected dispatch window, you may request to cancel your order and receive a refund instead of waiting.

5. Returns

Our Returns & Refunds Policy applies to all pre‑orders. You may be entitled to a repair, replacement or refund under the Australian Consumer Law if your tee is faulty, damaged or not as described. For details, please see our Refunds page. [web:35]

6. Contact
